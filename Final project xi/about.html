ABOUT QFAB

We introduce our self to inform you that we, serving all our customers since 2014 as a single window for all kinds of machine manufacturing and fabrication of various types, general Engineering and machine tool parts as per customer requirements. We have vast experience in the manufacturing of CNC, Printing machine and metal parts of heavy vehicles. Also we have 20 years of experience in various departments like heavy and light sheet metal fabrication, Tool room, Machine shop, Production, Process engineering, Man management, Design development, Assembly, QA, Testing, Application and Purchase. We have well equipped machine shop with advanced cutting and shearing machine, heavy flypress sander machine and conventional machining setup for making proto and developing components as per the requirement of our customers. Our technical team is well experianced and understands the customer drawings and requirement specification. We are the manfacturers of sheetmetal outercoverings for heavy machines cabinets, etc and also special tools work holdings and development of patterns and castings as per the customer requirements.
